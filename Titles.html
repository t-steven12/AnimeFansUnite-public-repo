ADD NEW/DISPLAY/UPDATE Titles

The Titles page regulates the Titles table.

This page displays a table of all available Anime Titles and their associated Artists.
An Anime Title can be added by submitting the Anime Title and the name of the Artist.
For a Title to be successfully added, the entered Artist must exist in the database.
The Artist of a Title can be updated by entering the desired Title and Artist in the
"Update Artist" form. The relationship between Titles and Artists is NULLable.
If the administrator/user does not enter an Artist upon adding a new Title or updating an
existing Title, the Artist for that Title will be NULL.
